Preliminary Due Diligence

A compliance anti-money-laundering officer reviews investor documents confidentially within one working day, requiring only a passport. Immigrant Invest, among the few licensed agents with a compliance department, rejection risks to just 1%.

  • Step 1
  • 1 day

Preparation of Documents

Immigrant Invest’s lawyers compile required personal and financial documents, including passports, certificates, and
bank statements. They complete state forms and certify copies, requiring only the investor’s signature.

  • Step 2
  • 2 to 4 weeks

Due Diligence

We submit the application and required documents to the government for the citizenship or residency by investment program. After the fee is paid, requiring checks for all family members over the age of 16 included.

  • Step 3
  • 2 to 6 months

Fulfilment of The Investment Condition

When the government department notifies Immigrant Invest
that the application has been approved, the investor has to fulfil the investment condition, usually a contribution to the state fund or buying real estate, within 90 days.

  • Step 4
  • Up to 3 months

Obtaining a Passport

The investor’s passport and naturalization certificate are usually produced within 4 weeks of the fulfilment of the investment condition. They are delivered to the address indicated by the investor in the application.

  • Step 5
  • 4+ weeks
